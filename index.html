Step 1 — Create your free Zapier account

Zapier connects this app to LinkedIn. It takes about 3 minutes and is free.

Open Zapier.com ↗

Step 2 — Create a Zap

  1. In Zapier click Create Zap
  2. Trigger: choose Webhooks by Zapier → Catch Hook
  3. Copy the webhook URL it gives you
  4. Action: choose LinkedIn → Create Share Update
  5. Map the text field to the webhook body
  6. Turn the Zap on

For scheduled posts: Use the same Zap. When you schedule a post, Agent 2 sends the text + scheduled time to Zapier. Zapier posts it at exactly that time automatically.
